Committed to quality and innovation in the Acacia Honey industry

PT. Victara Multi Parna is a leading producer of premium-quality acacia honey, dedicated to delivering natural and authentic honey products to consumers worldwide. With a commitment to sustainable practices and innovation, we take pride in harvesting the finest acacia honey through environmentally responsible methods.

Our mission is to provide pure, high-grade honey that meets international standards while supporting the well-being of local communities and preserving biodiversity. PT. Victara Multi Parna strives to be a trusted name in the honey industry, ensuring customer satisfaction through quality, transparency, and excellence.
