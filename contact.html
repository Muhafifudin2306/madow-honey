Authentic Indonesian Acacia Honey

  • Product Name: Authentic Indonesian Acacia Honey
  • Main Ingredient:
    • 100% pure honey derived from the nectar of Acacia tree flowers grown in Indonesia’s pristine forests.
    • Free from artificial additives, preservatives, and refined sugar.
  • Origin: Harvested from Riau, Sumatra, and Kalimantan.
  • Color: Light golden to amber.
  • Texture: Smooth and slightly runny.
  • Flavor Profile: Mildly sweet with subtle floral notes.
  • Aroma: Delicate floral fragrance.
  • Nutritional Content:
    • Rich in natural sugars (fructose and glucose).
    • Contains essential vitamins (B-complex, C) and minerals (calcium, potassium, magnesium).
    • Antioxidants for health benefits.
  • Moisture Content: Low (<20%).
  • Packaging Options:
    • Retail: Glass jars, squeeze bottles, or plastic containers (250g, 500g, 1kg).
    • Bulk: Food-grade drums (25kg–200kg).

  • International Standards Compliance: Certified by HACCP, ISO, and BPOM.
